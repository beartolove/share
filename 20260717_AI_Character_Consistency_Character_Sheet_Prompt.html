AI 角色一致性：Character Sheet Prompt

建議工具：ChatGPT Image 2 或 Nano Banana Pro

重點摘要

  • 這則貼文分享一個「角色設定表」prompt，用來幫 AI 圖像與 AI 影片建立同一角色的視覺一致性。
  • Prompt 要求輸出多角度角色 sheet：頭部正側面、全身正側背面、俯視／仰視、半身側面與材質細節。
  • 關鍵不是只生成漂亮角色，而是把臉部特徵、膚色、身高、體型、服裝、配件、光線與材質全部鎖定一致。
  • 全身區塊特別要求 orthographic projection 與 no perspective distortion，適合做成後續 AI 影片或多張圖的 reference。
  • 可用在 ChatGPT Image 2 或 Nano Banana Pro；同一結構也能換成寫實、3D Pixar 等不同風格。

Prompt 結構拆解

Head Details

頭部正面／側面、無配件與完整穿搭版本，用來鎖定臉部特徵。

Full Body Orthographic

5 個全身角度水平排列，確保身高、比例、服裝與背面資訊一致。

Supplementary Views

半身側面、俯視、仰視，補足一般 turnaround sheet 缺少的角度。

Material Detail

布料、鞋靴與材質特寫，保留後續生成時容易漂移的紋理細節。

Consistency Requirements

明確要求同一角色、同一光線、同一材質、無裁切、無重疊、標準化網格。

可複製 Prompt

Character Sheet Prompt
Professional character design sheet with realistic photography style, pure white background, 4K resolution. The character the following features:

HEAD DETAILS SECTION:

Front view of head without accessories

Right side view of head without accessories

Front view of head with full outfit and accessories

Right side view of head with full outfit and accessories
All head views must have identical facial features, skin tone, proportions, and no perspective distortion.

FULL BODY ORTHOGRAPHIC SECTION:
5 full body views arranged horizontally:

Front view

Left 45-degree side view

Right side view

Right 45-degree side view

Back view
All views must be equal width and height, orthographic projection with no perspective distortion.

SUPPLEMENTARY VIEWS SECTION:

Half-body side view

Top-down overhead view

Bottom-up under view
Character, outfit, and proportions must match main views exactly

MATERIAL DETAIL SECTION:
Extreme close-up shots of:

Fabric texture of clothing

Shoe and boot details
Clear display of material patterns, textures, and details

OVERALL CONSISTENCY REQUIREMENTS:

All modules show the same character with identical facial features, height, body type, outfit, accessories, lighting, and materials

High precision, maximum detail, cinematic animation character design standard

Professional photography lighting with soft shadows

Clean white background

No overlapping or cropping of modules

Clear and standardized layout

The design sheet should be organized in a grid layout with clear spacing between sections, professional character design sheet format.
