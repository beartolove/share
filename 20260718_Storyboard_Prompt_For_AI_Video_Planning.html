影片生成前先做 Storyboard：專業分鏡表 Prompt

建立時間：2026-07-18 17:59:58（Asia/Taipei）

#AI影片#Storyboard#Prompt#分鏡表

重點摘要

  • 這則貼文提醒：在生成影片前，先用 storyboard 把故事、鏡頭、節奏與轉場規劃清楚。
  • Prompt 要求輸出成 9:16 直式專業分鏡表，看起來像製片前期的 production planning document。
  • 版面由四格 header、log line、15 格 panel grid 與 footer notes 組成，能同時管理故事、鏡頭與風格一致性。
  • 每格分鏡都要包含 shot type、camera、action、notes/transition，適合拿來銜接 AI 影片生成或團隊溝通。
  • 最重要的價值是：先把「角色一致性、鏡頭節奏、情緒 tone、轉場」定義好，再進入影片生成，失敗率會更低。

分鏡表 Prompt 的核心結構

1. Header
先定義 project、style、tone、duration 與 aspect ratio。
2. Log line
用一句話說清楚故事主軸，避免鏡頭只是漂亮但沒有敘事。
3. Panel grid
以 5×3、15 格左右規劃，每格約 1–1.5 秒。
4. Metadata
每格固定填 shot type、camera、action、transition，便於後續生成與剪輯。

完整 Prompt（可複製）

Create a professional film storyboard infographic sheet, designed like a production planning document, 9:16 vertical aspect ratio, clean beige/cream paper background with thin black grid borders separating every section.

TOP HEADER BAR (4 boxes across):

Box 1: “PROJECT:” label with bold title “[YOUR PROJECT TITLE]” in large black sans-serif caps.

Box 2: “STYLE:” label with style description, e.g. “[YOUR VISUAL STYLE, e.g. paper craft / stop motion look, layered paper diorama]”.

Box 3: “TONE:” label with one word tone, e.g. “FUNNY” / “EMOTIONAL” / “SUSPENSEFUL”.

Box 4: “DURATION:” total seconds and “ASPECT RATIO:” ratio.

LOG LINE BAR: full-width strip below header reading “LOG LINE:” followed by a one-sentence story summary in italics.

PANEL GRID: 5 columns x 3 rows = 15 numbered panels (or adjust count to match total duration, roughly 1-1.5 seconds per panel).

Each panel must contain:

• A small numbered black tab (01, 02, 03...) top-left of the thumbnail image.

• Duration label top-right (e.g. “1.0 SEC” or “1.5 SEC”).

• A rendered still image inside the panel matching the chosen art style, showing the described action, characters rendered consistently across all panels (same character design, color, texture every frame).

• Below the image, a small metadata block with these exact labeled rows:

SHOT TYPE (e.g. WS – ESTABLISHING SHOT, CU – CLOSE UP, ECU – EXTREME CLOSE UP, OTS – OVER THE SHOULDER, MS – MEDIUM SHOT, LA CU – LOW ANGLE CLOSE UP)

CAMERA: (STATIC / SLIGHT PUSH IN / DYNAMIC TRACK / SHAKE-HANDHELD / FAST PUSH IN / SLIGHT PAN FOLLOW)

ACTION: one short sentence describing what happens in this beat.

NOTES/TRANSITION: (CUT / HARD CUT / MATCH CUT / HOLD / HOLD-LOOP END)

FOOTER BAR (3 sections across the bottom):

Section 1 “VISUAL NOTES:” bullet list of 2-3 production/style consistency rules (e.g. material, texture, lighting notes matching your chosen style).

Section 2 “CAMERA & EDITING NOTES:” bullet list of 2-3 pacing/editing rules with a small camera icon.

Section 3 “LEGEND:” small icon key explaining CUT (scissors icon), MATCH CUT (arrow icon), HOLD (pause icon).

Overall look: clean infographic/document style, high legibility, small bold black sans-serif labels, muted color palette in the thumbnail images, consistent character design and lighting across all 15 panels, sharp thin grid lines, no watermark, no extra text outside the defined boxes.

適合怎麼用

  • 先把故事換成你的專案名稱、視覺風格、tone、總秒數與 log line。
  • 再逐格補上角色行動與鏡頭語言，確認人物造型、材質、光線在 15 格中一致。
  • 最後把完成的 storyboard 作為影片生成 prompt、團隊討論稿或剪輯節奏表。
